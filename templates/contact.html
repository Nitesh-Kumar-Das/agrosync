About AgriSync

Who We Are

We are a team of passionate B.Tech students leveraging technology to support sustainable agriculture. Our goal is to provide farmers with real-time insights and smart recommendations to enhance productivity and resilience against climate change.

AgriSync integrates advanced Machine Learning, Data Analytics, and Web Technologies to deliver accurate and user-friendly solutions tailored to farmers' needs.
